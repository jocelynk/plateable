DishOut

Takeout and delivery containers generate massive amounts of waste. Every plastic dish thrown away increases our environmental footprint. DishOut wants to change this.

We save the environment one container at a time.

ABOUT

With our system, when consumers order takeout they will be provided with a reusable container that will be cleaned and returned to the restaurants through our service. Restaurants will save money on wasteful paper goods and foodies can live more sustainable lives while getting promotions from their favorite restaurants. Everyone Wins!

How it Works

Eat with our reusable container

Return at a nearby drop site
