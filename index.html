Plateable

We save the environment one plate at a time.

Takeout and Delivery containers generate massive amounts of waste every year. Every plastic dish thrown away contributes not only to overflowing landfills, but also increases our environmental footprint. Plateable wants to change this. With our system, when consumers order takeout they will be provided with a reusable container that can be returned at drop off points in their building, neighborhood, or office complex. Restaurants will save money on wasteful paper goods and foodies can live more sustainable lives while getting promotions from their favorite restaurants. Everyone Wins!
